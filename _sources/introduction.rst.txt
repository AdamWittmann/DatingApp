Introduction
============


This is project is a dating app design specifically for people who want to sort their through their options with superficial filters.

Installation
------------
dowlaod DatingApp file

open application when complete


Getting Started
---------------


Upon opening the application you will be greeting with a login page.

If you do not already have an account there is a prompt at the bottom that will direct you to the sign up page.

Here you can register and go through the account setup process. This involves entering you job information, you physical features, etc.

once completed you will be able to use the mian page to filter through our other users and find love!
